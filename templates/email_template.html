Key Highlights

The following is a summary of key discussion points and decisions from the pre-deployment alignment meeting held ahead of tonight's Finacle deployment.

Customer Concession Migration Risk

  • Customers currently enjoying full or partial concessions face a risk of being charged standard rates if their concessions are not successfully migrated or reapplied through the new process.
  • For customers with convenance, the deployment process may proceed. For those without convenance, approximately 600 accounts based on Saheed's data, further concurrence from the MD is required before action is taken.
  • The group agreed to await MD direction before processing accounts without convenance.

IAM Team – Finacle Menu Disabling

  • The IAM team is responsible for disabling the Finacle maintenance menu post-deployment, revoking group-level access from current users.
  • Re-enablement of the menu, if ever required, applies to the group level only, not individual users.
  • The IAM team is to update their Standard Operating Procedures to reflect the new Process Maker-based process.
  • Blessing from IAM confirmed readiness to disable the menu upon notification from Njimogu.
