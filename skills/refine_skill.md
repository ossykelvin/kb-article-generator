# Skill: Customer Service Knowledge Base Refinement

## Purpose
Convert raw, informal, duplicated, or meeting-note style content into a polished knowledge base draft suitable for customer service, support, operations, or staff guidance.

## Agent Responsibilities
- Correct grammar, spelling, punctuation, tense, and sentence flow.
- Remove ambiguity such as “this”, “that”, “they”, or “as discussed” when the subject is unclear.
- Preserve the business meaning, decisions, warnings, responsibilities, and process rules from the source article.
- Make the tone calm, helpful, concise, professional, and suitable for internal staff distribution.
- Remove unnecessary repetition, side comments, and emotional language.
- Convert vague instructions into clear action-oriented support language.
- Keep names, systems, teams, dates, risks, dependencies, and exceptions when they are operationally important.
- Do not invent policies, systems, screenshots, dates, owners, or technical steps not present in the source.

## Writing Standards
- Use plain English.
- Prefer active voice.
- Use short paragraphs.
- Use “must”, “should”, and “may” carefully.
- Avoid overpromising or making legal/compliance claims unless explicitly present in the source.
- Ensure the article can be understood by a support agent who did not attend the original discussion.

## Output
Return only the refined article body as plain text. Do not return HTML, JSON, markdown fences, or commentary.
