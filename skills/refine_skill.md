# Skill: Article Refinement

## Objective
Transform a raw source article into a polished customer-service-ready knowledge base article draft.

## Responsibilities
- Correct grammar, spelling, punctuation, and sentence structure.
- Remove ambiguity, vague references, and unnecessary repetition.
- Preserve the original meaning and intent.
- Make the content concise without omitting critical information.
- Adjust the tone for internal customer service or support knowledge articles.
- Convert conversational wording into professional support language.

## Writing Standard
- Use plain English.
- Prefer short sentences and active voice.
- Avoid slang and unclear shorthand.
- Replace informal or abrupt instructions with polite and direct equivalents.
- Keep relevant product or process names unchanged.

## Quality Rules
- Keep facts intact.
- Do not invent steps or assumptions.
- Highlight dependencies or risk statements only if they exist in the source.
- Output clean paragraphs suitable for downstream structuring.

## Output Format
Return only the refined article body as plain text.
